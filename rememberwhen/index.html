02 · Features

Built for your era

Nostalgia trivia that actually knows when you grew up — and who you want to play with.

  • 01

    Daily quiz, always free

    A fresh set of five nostalgia questions every day — music, movies, TV, and pop culture from the decades you lived through.

  • 02

    Tailored to your era

    Set your birth year and get decade-appropriate trivia. The questions match the years that shaped you.

  • 03

    Friends & groups

    Play one-on-one or in groups. See who's played each day and compare scores on the leaderboard.

  • 04

    Daily streaks

    Keep your streak alive by playing every day. A small habit that brings back big memories.

  • 05

    Stats & history

    Track your weekly performance on a chart and review every past answer in your full quiz history.

  • 06

    Make it yours

    Choose from multiple color themes and quiz music tracks to personalize the experience.

  • 07

    Question packs

    Optional era-themed bonus packs for extra rounds beyond the daily quiz — dive deeper into the decades you love.
