Remember When

A free daily nostalgia quiz for the decades you lived through. Five fresh questions every morning — music, movies, TV, and the moments that shaped your life.

🎵 Music 🎬 Movies 📺 TV 🌟 Events 🛍️ Products 1950s – 1980s
What ships at launch

Everything in v1.

Remember When is built to be a small daily ritual — quick to play, satisfying to return to, and better with friends.

🎯

The daily quiz

Five multiple-choice questions every day, drawn from a rotating set across the 1950s through the 1980s. One round per day — come back tomorrow for a new set.

📊

Scores & streaks

Earn up to 500 points per round. Track your current streak, personal best, and a seven-day score chart on your home screen.

👥

Friends circle

Share your invite code or send a link to add friends. See how everyone scored today, send a nudge when they haven't played, and keep each other honest.

🔔

Daily reminders

Set a reminder time that works for you. Optional nudge notifications when friends tap you to play. Turn either on or off in settings.

🎵

Sound & atmosphere

Light quiz music and sound effects while you play — with a toggle in settings if you prefer silence.

📱

iOS & Android

A native mobile app built for phones and tablets. Sign up with email or jump in as a guest and create an account later.

How it works

Your morning ritual.

Takes about two minutes. The same five questions for everyone, every day.

Step 01

Open & play

Tap Play on the home screen. Answer five nostalgia questions before the day gets away from you.

Step 02

See your score

Get instant feedback on each answer. Finish the round, celebrate the wins, and lock in your daily score.

Step 03

Keep the streak

Play again tomorrow to extend your streak. Miss a day and it resets — but your best score stays.

Step 04

Compare with friends

Check the friends circle to see who played and who needs a nudge. A little friendly competition never hurt.
