This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Remember When mobile application ("App") on iOS or Android.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Who we are

Remember When is operated by SoberSend, an individual business based in Washington, USA. SoberSend is not yet a formal LLC; this policy applies to the App as currently operated.

For privacy questions or requests, contact: sobersend@gmail.com

2. Who can use the App

The App is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

3. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: email address, password (stored securely by our authentication provider), and display name you choose during sign-up.
  • Profile information: display name and avatar color preference.
  • Social information: friend connections, friend invite codes you share or enter, and the status of invites you send (e.g., pending or accepted).
  • Gameplay information: daily quiz scores, number of correct answers, streaks, and related progress data.
  • Support communications: information you send us if you contact support.

Information collected automatically

  • App usage and diagnostics: if enabled, crash reports and error logs (e.g., via Sentry) to help us fix bugs. This may include device type, operating system version, and general app state at the time of an error.
  • Local preferences: sound/music settings, notification preferences, and similar choices stored on your device.

Information we do not collect

We do not currently collect:

  • Payment or billing information (the App is free; in-app purchases are not active in the initial release).
  • Precise GPS location.
  • Your phone's contact list, photos, or microphone recordings.
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted advertising.

4. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide the daily quiz, scores, streaks, and leaderboards among friends.
  • Enable friend invites and social features.
  • Send local daily reminder notifications on your device (if you enable them).
  • Improve reliability, security, and performance of the App.
  • Respond to support requests and enforce our Terms of Service.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only in these situations:

  • Service providers who help us run the App (see Section 6).
  • Other users you connect with: your display name, avatar, daily score (when you have played), and streak may be visible to friends you add in the App.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business changes: if SoberSend is reorganized, sold, or merged, information may transfer as part of that transaction (you will be notified where required by law).

7. Data storage and security

  • Account and gameplay data are stored on Supabase infrastructure (hosted in regions selected for our project).
  • Passwords are handled by Supabase Auth; we do not store plain-text passwords.
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

8. Data retention

We retain your information while your account is active and as needed to provide the App. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain certain records for legal, security, or backup purposes.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

In the App: you can update your display name in Settings and control sound, music, and daily reminder notifications.

Account deletion: you may request account deletion by emailing sobersend@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process verified requests within a reasonable time.

Washington residents may have additional rights under applicable state law. Contact us to exercise your rights.

10. Notifications

If you enable Daily Reminder, the App schedules a local notification on your device. Reminder time and on/off status are stored on your device and in app preferences. We do not use notifications to send marketing messages from our servers in the initial release.

11. International users

The App is operated from the United States. If you use the App from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. and other countries where our service providers operate.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
