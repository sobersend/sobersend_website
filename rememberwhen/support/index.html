Frequently asked questions

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab and tap the add button. You can share your personal invite code (it looks like RMBR-XXXX) or send an invite link by text or email. Friends can also enter your code on the Invite Friends screen. Once they accept, you'll see each other's daily scores.

What are the daily quiz rules?

Each day you get five multiple-choice questions drawn from nostalgia trivia — music, movies, TV, events, and products from the 1950s through the 1980s. You can complete the official daily quiz once per calendar day. Your score (up to 500 points) and streak update when you finish. Come back tomorrow for a fresh set.

How do I delete my account?

Email sobersend@gmail.com from the address linked to your account and ask us to delete it. We'll verify the request and remove your personal data within a reasonable time. You can also stop using the app at any time without contacting us.
