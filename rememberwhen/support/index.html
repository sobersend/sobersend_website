Frequently asked questions

The answers you need.

A few helpful details about playing, connecting with friends, and managing your account.

How does the daily quiz work?

Every day you get five multiple-choice nostalgia questions tailored to your era. You can complete the official daily quiz once per calendar day. Your score and streak update when you finish.

How do I add friends?

Open the Friends tab and tap the add button. Share your personal invite code or invite link. Once your friend accepts, you’ll be able to compare daily scores.

How do Groups work?

Create or join a Group using an invite link. Group members can see who has played each day and compare scores together on the leaderboard.

Can I review past quizzes?

Yes. Your quiz history lets you revisit past results and review your answers. You can also follow your recent performance in the weekly stats chart.

How do I change my theme or music?

Open Settings in the app to choose from the available color themes and quiz music tracks. Your selections can be changed whenever you like.

Is Remember When available on Android?

Yes. Remember When is available now on both iPhone and Android.

How do I delete my account?

Email sobersend@gmail.com from the address linked to your account and ask us to delete it. We’ll verify your request and remove your personal data within a reasonable time.
